VoxClaw

Give OpenClaw a voice

OpenClaw is the open-source personal AI agent that runs on your machine — your files, your shell, your messaging apps. VoxClaw gives it a voice. Run VoxClaw on your Mac and hear OpenClaw speak to you with high-quality text-to-speech: Apple's built-in voices out of the box, or OpenAI's neural voices when you want that extra polish. Paste text, pipe from the CLI, or send it over the network from another machine — and listen.
